FOOD STALL : KOLO MEE

Nestled in the heart of Nanyang Poly, the Kolo Mee Corner is a cherished food stall, offering students and staff a delicious escape into the flavors of Sarawak.
Founded by a passionate chef, this unassuming spot has quickly become a go-to for its authentic Kolo Mee. The aroma of sizzling noodles and fragrant shallot oil fills the air as students gather between classes to enjoy a comforting bowl. The menu caters to diverse tastes, providing classic options like minced pork or exciting twists for adventurous palates.
The Kolo Mee Corner isn't merely about satisfying hunger; it's about creating moments. Be it the comforting familiarity of a classic Kolo Mee bowl or the excitement of trying a new twist, students find solace and joy in this flavorful oasis within the bustling campus.
